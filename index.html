SPIRITUAL JOY

Becoming a Christian doesn't mean you have to be a boring person. You can still have fun and enjoy life in Christ. But we often don't hear about the fun things that Christians get to enjoy. Christians get to experience a joy that others outside of Christ do not get to experience. It is a joy that is unspeakable!

Being a Christian is the best decision you could ever make in your life. Jesus doesn't want you to live a strict religious life void of joy and happiness. He wants you to enjoy life and life more abundantly in Him without compromising your spiritual beliefs.

So stop being a fuddy-duddy and go have some good clean fun!

FUN 4 KIDS

Being a Christian child doesn't mean you have to give up your freedom to have fun with your friends. Check out our Top 3 Favorite Fun Things To Do with your friends.

  • Movie Night. Discuss and choose a movie that will be appropriate for you and your friends to enjoy!

  • Book Night. Discuss and choose your favorite book to dramatize in front of your friends. Everyone can get into the act!

  • Music Night. Discuss and put together a playlist of your favorite music to listen to. Let your inner DJ out!

FUN 4 ADULTS

As a Christian adult, we should never stop having a childlike attitude towards life and before God. Check out our Top 3 Favorite Fun Things To Do that will bring out the Big Kid in you!

  • Road Trip. Be spontaneous! Get some friends together and go take a day trip to a place you have always wanted to visit.

  • Pot Luck Night. Get your eats on by sampling food that you have wanted to taste but was afraid to try. Take lots of reaction photos!

  • Throwback Night. Go back in time and dress up in your favorite outfit or fashion from that era and show it off!

FUN 4 FAMILIES

Spending time with family should be fun not a chore! Just like we enjoy being in the presence of God, we should enjoy being together as a family. Check out our Top 3 Favorite Fun Things To Do together as a family.

  • Game Night. Who doesn't enjoy a good board game? The competition to win could be fierce but fun!

  • Charity Day. What better way to show family unity by helping one another and others. Spend the day doing something nice for one another and for those in need!

  • Dream Night. Be bold. Find a way to get an autograph from your favorite celebrity or see them perform. Make it happen!
